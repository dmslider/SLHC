Do I need an appointment?

Yes, all visa and passport services require an appointment. General inquiries can be made during office hours.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash, bank transfers, and debit cards. Credit cards are not accepted.

Can I visit without an appointment?

For general inquiries, yes. For services, appointments are mandatory.

What should I bring?

Bring your passport, completed forms, and any required supporting documents.
