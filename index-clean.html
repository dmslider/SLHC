About Our Mission

The Embassy of the Republic of Sierra Leone in Ireland was established to represent the interests of Sierra Leone and its citizens in the Republic of Ireland. We are committed to fostering strong bilateral relations and providing excellent consular services.

Our mission extends beyond traditional diplomatic functions to include promoting trade, investment, tourism, and cultural exchanges between Sierra Leone and Ireland.
