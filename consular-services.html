Consular Services

We provide a wide range of consular services to Sierra Leonean citizens and foreign nationals. All services are by appointment only.

1. Document Notarization

Official certification of documents for use in Sierra Leone or internationally.

  • Affidavits and statutory declarations
  • Powers of attorney
  • Consent letters
  • Certified copies of documents
  • Witness signatures

Fee: ‚Ç¨30 per document | Processing: Same day

2. Birth Certificate Services

Registration and certification of births for Sierra Leonean citizens.

  • Birth registration for children born abroad
  • Duplicate birth certificates
  • Birth certificate authentication

Required Documents:

  • Hospital birth notification or certificate
  • Parents' passports and IDs
  • Marriage certificate (if applicable)
  • Two passport photos of the child

Fee: ‚Ç¨50 | Processing: 2-3 weeks

3. Marriage Certificate Services

Registration and authentication of marriages.

  • Marriage certificate registration
  • Duplicate certificates
  • Certificate authentication for foreign use

Fee: ‚Ç¨60 | Processing: 2-3 weeks

4. Death Certificate Services

Registration and authentication of death certificates.

  • Death certificate registration
  • Duplicate certificates
  • Certificate authentication

Fee: ‚Ç¨50 | Processing: 2-3 weeks

5. Authentication & Legalization

Official stamp and seal for documents to be used in Sierra Leone.

  • Educational certificates and transcripts
  • Professional qualifications
  • Police clearance certificates
  • Medical certificates
  • Business documents
  • Court documents

Fee: ‚Ç¨40 per document | Processing: 3-5 days

6. Police Clearance Certificate

Certificate of good conduct for Sierra Leonean citizens.

  • Application form
  • Passport copy
  • Fingerprints (taken at mission)
  • Two passport photographs
  • Proof of residence

Legal authorization for someone to act on your behalf in Sierra Leone.

  • Valid passport
  • Details of attorney (full name, address, ID)

  • Personal appearance required
